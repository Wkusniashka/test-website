Hello World!

I’m Anastasiia and I really excited to apply for Founders and Coders summer course. Programming is new for me but I'm already fascinated with endless possibilities it provides. It gives me a lot of joy to be able to create or change things with words. Every task is like a riddle that has a lot of different solutions and it's up to you what path you take. I recently moved to London after studying in Medical University in Poland but lately I started to look for opportunities for the career change. That's why I thought that this course will be perfect for me. I’m new to the city and I was overwhelmed with amount of choices it provides but when I found Founders and Coders I understood that this is it. I really like F&C approach to learning. Being a Medical Student I had to do a lot of studying and it can be isolating if you are studying by yourself. I strongly believe that you can be much more efficient when you studying in a group. It builds a strong community and helps to look at a problem from different perspectives. I’m serious about starting career in a web development and I’m looking for something I can devote myself fully and not just for a couple days experience. I like to learn and I have a lot of experience with self-learning. I’m used to remembering a big pieces of information and study every day for a long time. I truly believe that you can learn something only by doing a piece of work every day. Sometimes you think that you’re not moving forward but in the long run you are really grateful for all the work you did every day and see that it really matters.
